Our Flagship Product

Famaloo

Family medication tracking · iOS & Android

Famaloo is a family medication tracking app that helps households coordinate medications across every family member. Adults can share schedules, log doses, and quietly hand off caregiving to one another — whether they're managing a parent's prescriptions, a partner's routine, or their own. Built by Crafty Codr Inc. and published to both the Apple App Store and Google Play.

How we work.

A decade of shipping software for other people has taught us what matters — and what doesn't. These are the principles we run every engagement on.

01 · Discover

Understand before building.

We start every project with a short, paid discovery. You leave with a written architecture, a realistic scope, and a decision on whether we're the right team — before you sign anything bigger.

02 · Build

Senior engineers, every week.

The person you meet is the person writing your code. Small teams, short feedback loops, and a working production deploy from the very first week of development.

03 · Hand off

Code your team can own.

Boring technology, readable code, and the kind of documentation we'd want inherited ourselves. When we're done, your team is equipped to keep shipping without us.

About Crafty Codr.

Founded in 2014 and headquartered in Kitchener, Ontario, Crafty Codr Inc. is an independent software studio owned and operated by engineers. We stay small on purpose: fewer projects, deeper attention, longer relationships. Past work spans healthtech, fintech, SaaS platforms, and consumer mobile — and our own flagship product, Famaloo, is live on both major mobile platforms.
